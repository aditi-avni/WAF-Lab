About the Event

GITAM TechFest is an annual technical and cultural festival where students showcase their creativity, technical skills and innovation. The fest includes coding competitions, hackathons, robotics, workshops and cultural performances.

Highlights

  • National Level Coding Contest
  • 24-Hour Hackathon
  • Robotics Challenge
  • Technical Workshops
  • Dance & Music Night
  • Photography Competition
